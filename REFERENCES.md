# What people say about working with me

These are full-length recommendations from former colleagues and direct reports who worked with me at StudyWay and Matrius. They describe the same themes from different positions: bringing structure to ambiguity, making ownership clear, staying calm under pressure, investing in people's growth and leaving behind a system the team can continue using.

The recommendations were written in Russian. Faithful English translations are provided first for international readers, followed by the original wording. Nothing here was generated from a questionnaire answered by me; these are the authors' own accounts of our work together.

Published with each author's permission. Private contact details are intentionally omitted from this public page.

> "Rustem can bring order to the messiest processes fast - and he showed me by his own example what a genuinely invested head of marketing looks like. After he left, I was promoted to Head of Marketing on the system he built."

### Full English translation

I had the good fortune to work with Rustem when he was Chief Marketing Officer and I was Team Lead of Performance Marketing on his team.

Rustem is a manager who can quickly bring order to even the messiest, most unstructured processes. He builds a team's work systematically, defines clear areas of responsibility, and creates transparent processes so everyone understands their role and can deliver. At the same time he's genuinely invested in his people's growth: he regularly discussed career development, offered opportunities to learn, and trusted us with work that drew out the team's potential.

I'd also single out his depth of expertise and his constant drive to stay at the leading edge of the industry - new marketing tools, technologies, acquisition channels and best practices. Thanks to a wide professional network he always had current information and helped the team adapt quickly to change.

Working under him was an important stage in my professional growth. Rustem showed me by his own example what a good, genuinely invested head of marketing looks like. He helped me pick up acquisition channels I hadn't worked with before, taught me to work effectively with external contractors, and gradually handed me much more responsibility - which let me build my management skills and make strategic decisions more confidently.

After Rustem left, I was promoted to Head of Marketing, in large part thanks to the knowledge, management approaches and organising principles he'd built into the team. I carried on developing the marketing function on the system he created, and that experience is still valuable to me today.

I recommend Rustem without hesitation as a leader who combines strategic thinking, strong management expertise and a genuine investment in his team's success. Working with him is a comfortable, effective partnership that delivers results beyond expectations.

I went on to lead the social-studies EGE/OGE department at Shkolkovo, one of the largest online schools in Russia. I now teach exam-prep courses to more than 10,000 students in total and am one of the most in-demand teachers in the country.

I'd recommend Rustem because I never saw him face a problem he couldn't break down into a workable plan - from launching online courses to whatever came next.

## What these recommendations establish

Across five independent accounts, the recurring pattern is consistent:

- I bring structure to unclear or disorganised work;
- I make ownership, deadlines and standards visible;
- I remain accessible, calm and diplomatic when the work becomes difficult;
- I invest in people's learning and trust them with increasing responsibility;
- I build processes that remain useful after I leave;
- people who worked with me carried those management principles into later leadership roles.

These recommendations primarily validate my leadership and operator background.
