### What I work on

- **Production LLM pipelines** — turning messy real-world audio into structured, *honest* output, with
  guardrails against a model inventing things, cost tracked per call, and evals so a model swap can't
  quietly make it worse.
- **Speech-to-text at scale** — running a speech model on GPU workers: transcription, speaker separation,
  and identifying *who* is speaking by voice, not just "speaker A vs B."
- **The whole product surface** — iOS (Swift), Android (Kotlin), web (Next.js), backend (NestJS),
  built and shipped by one person.
- **An AI-orchestrated way of building** — a system of skills, review agents, and automated gates that
  lets a solo founder ship at the pace of a small team, without cutting corners.
